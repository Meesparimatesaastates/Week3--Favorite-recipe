Hot-smoked Salmon
Needed Ingredients / cooking-equipment
Component Quantity
Salmon filee 1 kg
Smoker 1pc
alder sawdust 20g
Aluminum Foil 40x40 cm 1 sheet
Spices
  • Seasalt
  • grounded black pepper
  • some dill
  • a couple of slices of lemon or citric acid
  • a little parsley
Preaparing / Cooking

Place the salmon on aluminum foil on a smoke rack and season with seasalt and pepper. Put the sawdust in the smoking pan and put the smoke oven on. Make sure that the temperature of the smoke oven does not rise more than 180 degrees. Smoking time is up to 35 minutes.

Serving food

Place the cooled dish on a plate, season with lemon juice. Garnish with a slice of lemon, a little green dill and parsley.

That's it, Bon Appetit!
